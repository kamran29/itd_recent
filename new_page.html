Our Mission

Why we started

Our mission at iTryDIY is to provide everything needed to empower people of all ages to create their own do-it-yourself (DIY) projects. We design each project kit to allow people to learn, develop, and use new skills to make a project that they can call their own. We strive to make DIY projects accessible, convenient, and fun in order to inspire more people to unleash their inner creativity through hands-on learning.

Our Values

What we stand for

Creativity

Creativity provides an outlet for personal expression that allows us to project what feels right on the “canvas” in front of us. For us, this means designing an experience that revolves around you. For you, this means the possibilities are endless!

Integrity

Achieving our mission depends solely on the integrity of our work. Being there for our community is what drives us to deliver quality experiences and as such, we aim to take our work to the next level!

Growth

Any experience that involves learning is an experience that will help us all grow. Our growth is derived from tackling new challenges and constantly iterating on our processes. How will you grow?
