Our project selection changes from time to time as we discover new ideas and develop DIY kits. Currently we offer 3 project kits: painting on canvas, creating a custom wood sign, and string art on wood. These kits yield projects that can be displayed as home decor, given as gifts to loved ones, or kept as a memory of something YOU created.
+
One of our kits can keep you entertained from anywhere from 1.5 to up to 3 hours or even more depending on the type of project and amount of detail and effort you want to put in to make it your own!
+
We have kits that can be used by practically any age group from young kids to adults! Our project kits have a suggested age group, but you can decide what will best for you or your child based on ability/age/parental supervision.

Yes you can! However, what makes iTryDIY different is that we provide the convenience of sourcing, preparing, and packaging all materials together that you would need for a specific project. No need to go out and shop for individual materials or order in large quantity packages. We will include everything you need to create a piece with little to no waste of materials. We also provide a step by step guide that tells you exactly how to use the supplies we provide to turn your DIY into a finished piece. We strive to make starting a DIY project as easy and convenient as possible!

At iTryDIY, we want to make access to DIY projects ready to available to as many people as we can. Therefore, we have priced our kits very fairly based on sourcing of quality supplies, preparation and packaging of kits, and delivery. Our DIY project kits range anywhere from $25-$35 for a single kit. Bundle options are available for families, parties, etc. If you are located within the DFW area, you qualify for FREE curbside home delivery.
